# TWM Glossary

Short definitions for club reading. Terms are drawn from [twm_canon_club_release_2026-07-01.md](twm_canon_club_release_2026-07-01.md). Where a term is marked *candidate* or *provisional* in canon, that is noted.

---

### Thicket

A local living software organism: bounded Information Field, executable symbionts, explicit truth authority, and flow maintained rather than frozen.

### Information Field

The bounded, stratified space in which a thicket's information exists, flows, gains or loses authority, is projected, collapses into residue, or decays into compost. Not a database.

### Source Field

Stage 0 before the Information Field: the wider material from which a thicket *selects* what becomes meaningful and operational inside its boundary.

### Truth

Role-bound and lifecycle-bound operational reliability: what a given part of the system may rely on, at a given moment, for a given purpose, under a given authority. Truth is inhabited, not merely stored.

### Fact / Fact cluster

A **fact** is an authority-bound decidable assertion. A **fact cluster** is the natural unit of shared authoritative acceptance. Only one authority decides a given fact.

### Domain truth / Operative truth

**Domain truth** names what the Field means. **Operative truth** names how a role participates. Each living role receives only the lawful slice of both.

### Tree

A canonical-authority symbiont that decides domain truth within a declared domain. Shapes lawful Shadows, Intents, refusals, and capabilities — but does not expose raw truth or perform mundane work.

### Symbiont

An executable participant inside the local organism: lifecycle, local operational truth, communication boundaries, and a role in event ecology. Authority differs by role (Tree, Avatar, Steward, Demiurge, etc.).

### Avatar

A human-facing symbiont: lawful living presence in relation with the system. Carries intellect and purpose; not replaceable by a Rational Symbiont alone.

### Demiurge

Lifecycle-authority symbiont: fulfills archetype, births and kills symbionts, supervises becoming, harvests remains. Owns **lifecycle truth**, not business truth.

### Membrane

Controlled boundary surface — the only legal crossing between a Thicket and its outside. Inbound traffic proposes; internal authority decides. No raw authority crosses a Membrane.

### Mycelium

Rooted internal relational layer of a living Thicket. Carries living relation inside one host. Does not leave the host and must not become an imperial cross-thicket dependency fabric.

### Shadow

Lawful projection of truth for a participant. A capability to perceive. Shows shaped truth but does not decide it. Must not become authority.

### SmallWorld / WholeWorld

A Symbiont's **SmallWorld** is the local state-world it may inhabit, perceive, remember, act within, and lawfully change. The Thicket's **WholeWorld** is its full Information Field.

### Capability / Posture / Capacity

**Capability** — specified lawful power in context (what a Symbiont can actually do, receive, call, emit, perceive, or use). **Posture** — runtime shape of a Symbiont; not the sum of Capabilities. **Capacity** (*candidate*) — what a being can bear, distinct from what it may lawfully perform.

### Archetype / Becoming

**Archetype** — declarative structural intent; inspectable, composable, reviewable. **Becoming** — the world does not change in place; it becomes anew through accepted archetype revision and Demiurge fulfillment.

### Compost / Waste

**Compost** — non-authoritative material preserved for future fertility (failures, observations, rejected structure). May nourish future form after digestion. **Waste** — compost that cannot nourish future form.

### Flow

Maintained movement of truth, events, capabilities, and lawful participation. A healthy thicket preserves flow rather than freezing structure.

### Flower / Meadow / Field

**Flower** — whole system experience of a thicket (living experience, not merely code or UI). **Meadow** — plurality of Flowers. **Field** — shared experiential world formed by those Flowers; must become beautiful, not merely stable.

### Beneficiary

Being or community whose life the system is meant to make better. Split into methodology-level and application-level beneficiary; application-level has priority.

### Panopticum

Governing posture seeking expanding legibility to a centre, treating visibility as basis for administration, prediction, ownership, extraction, and authority. TWM's outer negative boundary.

### Captured Technological Sovereignty

Technological and infrastructural form of Panopticum logic: infrastructure seizes the Information Field, dissolves boundaries, centralizes capability, obscures truth, and converts intention into extractable control. Main antithesis of TWM.

### Rational Symbiont

Bounded reasoning participant (e.g. an LLM). May function as an *alchemical vessel* (*candidate*) — holding complexity for transformation — but must not decide truth or bypass Tree authority.

### Sapling

Local draft or experimental truth held by an Avatar or similar; must not mutate canon directly.

### Steward / Monster

**Steward** — operational-power symbiont with one bounded executable responsibility. **Monster** — pathological or uncontrolled operational form; must not become sovereign.

### Event

Lawful signal of change in the Information Field. Proposals and effects are distinguished from canonical acceptance.

### Seasons of Software Life

Knowledge and structure have seasons: arrival, incarnation, flow, harvest, return to compost. Demiurge serves incarnation within the season; it does not own the season.

### Canon Tests

Nine adversarial tests for patterns entering canon: Truth Pressure, Fractal Nature, Stakeholder Transmission, Authority Mapping, Beneficiary Orientation, Seasonal Timing, Becoming Continuity, Four Causes (*candidate*), Capacity and Capability (*candidate*). Metabolic constraint applies across all.

### Cheating pattern

Failure mode where metaphor hides mechanism, projection becomes authority, local state becomes canon, transport changes ontology silently, or an LLM proposal becomes execution without Tree acceptance.
